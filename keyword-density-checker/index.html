💡 About This Tool

Keyword density shows how often a word appears relative to total word count. Ideal density is typically 1-3% for primary keywords.

🎯 Stop Words Explained

Stop words like "the", "and", "of" are common but carry little meaning. Removing them gives more accurate insights about your actual content topics.

📤 Export & Share

Use the CSV export to analyze data in Excel or Google Sheets. Perfect for SEO reports and content audits.
