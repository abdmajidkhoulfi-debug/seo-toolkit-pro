📌 Quick Tips

Best practices

✅ Use root-relative paths: /admin/

✅ Sitemap needs full URL: https://example.com/sitemap.xml

✅ Use / to block all crawling

✅ Add multiple groups for different bots

📖 robots.txt Limitations

Robots.txt controls crawling, not indexing. Use noindex meta tags or X-Robots-Tag headers to prevent indexing of sensitive pages.
