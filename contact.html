FAQ

Common questions

What should I use the contact form for?

You can use it for support questions, bug reports, feature suggestions, partnerships, and general inquiries.

How quickly will I get a reply?

Set clear response expectations on the contact page because that helps visitors feel their message will not disappear into a void. A short expectation window is a common trust-builder. [web:447][web:446]

Should I show direct email too?

Yes. A dedicated contact page should include direct ways to reach you, not just a form, because users often prefer different contact methods. [web:453][web:454]
